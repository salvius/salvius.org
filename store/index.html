The Robot Science Coloring Book

This coloring book features over 30 pages based on the robotic designs of Salvius. Page themes cover concepts around where raw materials used to build robots originate from, how various components are made, as well as various types of electronic components and what they are used for. From robotic arms used in factories, to humanoid robots designed to work alongside people, the world of robotics is intriguing and filled with new ideas to learn about.

Stand-up for Robots

In a world powered by algorithms and illuminated by LEDs, one robot stands before the microphone, searching not for power, but for laughter.

Salvius, is trying his hand as a comedian and has been learning the oldest form of human code: humor. From stage lights to system logs, his routines explore what it means to think, to feel, and exist in this absurd modern world.

Free Earth Day Game

Resource Rescue

A retro 32-bit style platformer game celebrating Earth Day, featuring Salvius navigating a world made of recycled materials.

Play through three unique levels exploring themes of recycling, environmental preservation, and clean energy. Built with Phaser 3, this open-source game works on desktop and mobile devices, with full accessibility features and offline support as a progressive web app.

Join our mission: Contribute to the game or sponsor Salvius Labs on GitHub to support open-source robotics projects focused on sustainability and education.
