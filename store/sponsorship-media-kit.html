About the Book

The Robot Science Coloring Book features over 30 pages based on authentic robotic designs and technical sketches. This educational coloring book introduces readers to real robotics and computer science concepts through creative exploration.

Topics covered include:

  • Raw materials and manufacturing processes for robot components
  • Electronic components and their functions
  • Robotic arms and industrial automation
  • Humanoid robots and human-robot collaboration
  • Computer science and programming concepts
