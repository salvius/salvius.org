Resource Rescue is a retro 32-bit style platformer game featuring Salvius, an open-source humanoid robot built from recycled materials. Created to celebrate Earth Day, this game takes players on a journey through three unique levels where environmental themes and sustainable practices are woven throughout the gameplay.

The Story

Salvius awakens in a desert landscape, missing crucial components. Navigate through challenging terrain to collect five essential parts: a battery, motor, wire roll, gear, and circuit board. Each level brings new challenges and reinforces the importance of resourcefulness and environmental stewardship.



Game Levels


Level 1: The Desert

Navigate the desert landscape using arrow keys or WASD controls. Jump over obstacles and collect 5 essential robot parts scattered across the terrain. Press Shift while moving to run faster!

Level 2: The City of Scrap

Navigate through a junkyard while avoiding rats that will damage your circuits. You have 3 lives (batteries) - jump over obstacles and rats to reach the radio tower at the end of the city.

Level 3: The Radio Tower

Switch to vertical platforming as you jump from platform to platform, climbing to the top of the radio tower to broadcast an important message about environmental preservation.
