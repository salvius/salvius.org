The Robot Science Coloring Book

by Gunther Cox

This coloring book features over 30 pages based on the robotic designs and sketches of Salvius. Page themes cover concepts around where the raw materials used to build robots originate from, how various components are made, as well as various types of electronic components and what they are used for. From robotic arms used in factories, to humanoid robots designed to work alongside people, the world of robotics is intriguing and filled with new ideas to learn about.
