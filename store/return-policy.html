Return Policy

Our printed book orders and returns are fulfilled by Amazon. We do not handle returns or refunds directly for products purchased through Amazon, and all of Amazon's return policies apply to our products sold and shipped through their services. All returns and refunds processed by Amazon are subject to their return policies, which may include restocking fees, return shipping costs, and other conditions as outlined on their website. We recommend reviewing Amazon's return policy before making a purchase to understand the terms and conditions that may apply to your order. For all orders placed through Amazon, their return policy will be the governing policy for returns and will supersede any return policies stated here on our website. We do not have any additional return policies beyond what is outlined by Amazon. If you have any issues with your order, please contact Amazon customer support for assistance.

For more information about returns, please visit Amazon's return policy page:
Amazon Return Policy.

Key Return Policy Details:
Per our last review of Amazon's return policy (2026-04-05), the following key details apply to our products sold through Amazon:

  • This policy applies to all products sold in the United States.
  • Returns are accepted for both defective and non-defective products.
  • Exchanges are generally limited to replacements for defective or damaged copies of the same title.
  • Items must be in new and unused condition to be eligible for a full refund.
  • Amazon provides return labels through their Returns Center; whether shipping is prepaid or deducted from your refund depends on the reason for the return.
  • Returns must be initiated within 30 days of receipt of the item.
  • Return shipping costs may apply, and restocking fees may be charged for certain returns.
  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method after the returned item is received and inspected by Amazon.
Please note that these details are subject to change based on Amazon's policies, and we recommend reviewing their return policy for the most up-to-date information.
